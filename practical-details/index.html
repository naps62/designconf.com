venue

Auditório Vita

Mirror Conf will take place in Braga, a beautiful, friendly city surrounded by stunning mountains and one of the oldest portuguese cities, established in Roman times as Bracara Avgvsta.

The venue for Mirror Conf will be Auditório Vita, a beautiful piece of architecture. Only 5 minutes away from downtown, this is the perfect place to welcome your creative minds. Find your way here!

Getting to Braga

Porto (OPO)

The closest airport is in Porto (OPO), from where you can either:

  1. catch a direct bus with service called GetBus
  2. catch a train at Porto Campanhã (~1 hour trip)

 

 

Lisbon (LIS)

If you want to land in Lisbon, you can catch a train at Lisboa Oriente to Braga (~4 hour trip).

flight discount

Our official carrier, TAP Portugal, is giving a discount code to all attendees. If you are traveling in tourist class you will have a 10% discount. If traveling in business class you will get 20% discount.

what to do in Braga

Braga is Portugal’s third largest city and it was the European Youth Capital in 2012. Braga’s tech scene exploded over the last 5 years, driven by a fantastic academic institution like Universidade do Minho and exciting initiatives like Startup Braga.

Only 30 minutes away from breathtaking Porto, we suggest you take the opportunity to stay a few more days and fall in love with Portugal. Don’t take our word for it, check out these 43 reasons why you’ll love it here.
